Why It Matters

Your Portfolio Website vs A Standard CV

A CV tells a hiring manager what you did. A portfolio website shows them exactly who you are, what you can do, and why you are worth hiring — all in one click.

📄 Standard CV / Resume

Forgettable in a Pile of 200

  • Static text — no visual impact or personality
  • Gets deleted or lost after one quick read
  • Cannot show actual work — only describes it
  • Formatting breaks across different devices
  • No way to demonstrate technical or creative skills
  • No links, no interactivity, no personality
  • Needs to be updated and re-sent manually
🌐 Personal Portfolio Website

Memorable, Shareable, Always Online

  • Visual, animated, and uniquely branded to YOU
  • Lives at a permanent URL — share it everywhere
  • Show real projects, galleries, and case studies
  • Looks perfect on every phone, tablet & desktop
  • Proves your skills by existing as a working website
  • Contact forms, social links & WhatsApp built-in
  • Update content once — visible to everyone instantly

What's Inside

Every Feature Your Portfolio Needs

Every portfolio I build includes these core sections and features — all designed to impress, perform, and convert visitors into opportunities.

Interactive Project Gallery

A visually rich, filterable gallery that lets visitors browse your work by category — with hover animations, project descriptions, and links to live demos or case studies.

  • Category filter buttons (Web, Design, Photography, etc.)
  • Smooth hover zoom and overlay effects
  • Project title, description, and external links
  • Mobile-optimised touch-friendly browsing

Personal Brand & About Section

A dedicated section that introduces you — your story, what you do, your skills, and what you stand for. Designed to leave a lasting impression in the first 10 seconds.

  • Professional profile photo placement
  • Animated skill bars or tag clouds

Contact Form & Social Integration

Make it easy for clients, employers, and collaborators to reach you. Multiple contact options so no opportunity slips through the cracks.

  • Working contact form delivered to your inbox

  • Linked social icons (LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram)
