How I Help

Avoiding the Common Mistakes That Cost Students Their Place

Every year, thousands of Grade 12 learners lose their spots at their dream institutions — not because of poor marks, but because of avoidable application errors. Here's how I make sure that doesn't happen to you.

The Trap

Incorrect APS Calculations

Many students calculate their Admission Point Score (APS) incorrectly — applying to programmes they don't qualify for, or underestimating themselves and missing out on better opportunities.

I cross-check your subject marks against the exact faculty prospectus for each institution, calculating your APS correctly and matching you to realistic, suitable programmes.

The Trap

Document Rejections

Blurry ID photos, incorrectly formatted files, wrong file sizes, or results submitted as images instead of PDFs — these errors cause applications to be rejected outright before they're even reviewed.

I ensure every document is scanned as a clear, high-quality PDF, correctly named, properly sized, and in the exact format each portal requires before uploading.

The Trap

Missed Deadlines

Popular programmes — like Nursing, Education, and Engineering — close months before others. Students who don't track each institution's closing date miss their window completely with no second chance.

I keep a live tracking list of closing dates for every portal and programme you're applying to, and remind you in advance so nothing is ever submitted too late.

What You Need

The Matriculant Checklist — What to Send Me

Before I can start your application, I need a few documents from you. Don't stress — most of these you already have. Here's exactly what to prepare and send via WhatsApp.

Application Document Checklist

Send these items via WhatsApp before we begin — photos are fine to start

Latest School Report

Your most recent academic report — Term 3 mid-year results are usually enough. Final results are needed for final submissions.

Your ID Copy

A clear, unobstructed copy of your South African ID document or smart card. Both sides of the smart card if applicable.

Parent / Guardian ID

Required by most portals for financial assessment. A clear photo of your parent or legal guardian's ID document is sufficient.

Valid Email Address

A working email that you can access — this is where institutions send your login details, offer letters, and student number.

Working WhatsApp Number

A phone number you check regularly. I'll send you updates, proof of submission, and application confirmations directly to WhatsApp.

Your Career Interests

Tell me what you want to study and why — even rough ideas help. If you're unsure, that's completely okay. We'll figure it out together.

Photo quality matters: Blurry, dark, or cut-off documents are the number one reason applications are rejected. Take photos in good lighting, ensure the whole document is visible, and avoid using older screenshots. I will tell you if something needs to be retaken before I submit anything.
