Before We Start

What Your Business Needs to Prepare

To build your website quickly and correctly, I'll need a few things from you. Don't worry — I'll guide you through each one, and some items are optional depending on your project.

Your Branding

Visual identity elements that make your website look "you".

  • Logo file (PNG with transparent background is best)
  • Brand colours (hex codes, or just describe them)
  • Preferred fonts (optional — I can suggest)
  • Any existing design guidelines or style preferences

Your Content

The words and images that fill your website.

  • Written text for each page (or bullet points — I can write it)
  • High-quality photos of your business, products, or team
  • Your business description ("About Us" text)
  • Product / service list with descriptions and prices (if applicable)

Domain Name Choice

Your website's address on the internet (e.g. www.yourbusiness.co.za).

  • Think of 2–3 options in case your first choice is taken
  • Keep it short, memorable, and related to your business name
  • Prefer .co.za for local SA businesses, .com for international
  • I can register it for you or guide you to do it yourself

Your Goals & Target Audience

Knowing who the site is for determines every design decision.

  • Who is your ideal customer? (age, location, needs)
  • What is the ONE main thing you want visitors to do?
  • Do you need bookings, enquiry forms, or online sales?
  • Any competitor websites you admire? (for style reference)

Business Contact Details

All the ways customers can reach you.

  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Business email address
  • Physical address or service area
  • Social media profile links (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)

Web Hosting (Optional)

Where your website lives on the internet. I handle this for you if needed.

  • I can recommend affordable South African hosting providers
  • Or I can host small sites for free using modern platforms
  • If you already have hosting, just share your login details securely
  • Monthly hosting costs will be explained upfront — no surprises
