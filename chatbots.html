What Your Chatbot Can Do

Every chatbot I build is custom-trained on your business — your products, your tone, your frequently asked questions.

FAQ & Knowledge Bot

Train your chatbot on your most common questions so customers get instant, accurate answers — no waiting, no frustration.

  • Answers business hours, pricing, and policies automatically
  • Trained on your specific product or service catalogue
  • Escalates to you when it can't answer
  • Learns and improves over time

Lead Generation Bot

Capture name, number, and interest from every visitor who interacts — then deliver those warm leads straight to your inbox or WhatsApp.

  • Collects contact details through natural conversation
  • Qualifies leads based on your criteria
  • Sends lead alerts directly to your phone
  • Integrates with your existing CRM or spreadsheet

Booking & Appointment Bot

Let customers book consultations, salon slots, deliveries, or services through a guided chatbot flow — no phone calls required.

  • Shows available time slots in real-time
  • Sends booking confirmation and reminders
  • Reduces no-shows through automated follow-ups
  • Works on website or WhatsApp

24/7 Customer Support Bot

A first-line support agent that handles complaints, tracks orders, and guides troubleshooting before any human intervention is needed.

  • Handles after-hours queries without delay
  • Provides step-by-step troubleshooting guidance
  • Logs unresolved issues for follow-up
  • Smooth handoff to human agent when needed

WhatsApp-Integrated Bot

Deploy your chatbot directly on WhatsApp — the platform your South African customers already use every single day.

  • Works within the WhatsApp interface — no new app needed
  • Sends automated welcome messages and menus
  • Handles enquiries, orders, and feedback
  • Connects to your WhatsApp Business account

Website-Embedded Bot

A floating chat widget on your website that greets every visitor, answers questions, and guides them toward a purchase or enquiry.

  • Matches your website colours and branding
  • Triggers welcome messages on page arrival
  • Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • One line of code to embed on any site
