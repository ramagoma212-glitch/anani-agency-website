Is It Time?

Signs Your Website Is Hurting Your Business

Most business owners don't realise their old website is costing them clients every day. Here are the three biggest warning signs — and how I fix each one.

Warning Sign

It Looks Like It's from 2010

Outdated fonts, clashing colours, cluttered layouts, and no clear brand identity. Visitors judge your credibility in under 3 seconds — and old design says "don't trust me."

I rebuild the visual identity from scratch — modern typography, clean colour palette, and a layout that feels premium and trustworthy.

Warning Sign

It Breaks on Mobile Phones

Over 70% of South African internet users browse on mobile. If your site is hard to use on a phone — tiny text, broken layouts, unclickable buttons — you are losing most of your visitors.

Every redesign I deliver is mobile-first — built to look and function perfectly on every screen size, from phones to widescreen monitors.

Warning Sign

Visitors Come but Never Take Action

Traffic but no enquiries, no calls, no sales. Your site lacks clear calls-to-action, a logical flow, and the trust signals that push visitors to actually contact you.

I redesign with conversion in mind — prominent contact buttons, persuasive copy placement, and a structure that guides visitors toward taking action.
